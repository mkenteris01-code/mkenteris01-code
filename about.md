# About Me

I am **Michael Kenteris**, a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [University of the Aegean](https://www.aegean.gr/), Department of Cultural Technology and Communication.

## Research Interests

- **GraphRAG & Knowledge Graphs** - Building structured memory systems for AI
- **Federated Learning** - Privacy-preserving machine learning
- **Large Language Models** - Applications in education and language learning
- **Neurosymbolic AI** - Combining neural networks with symbolic reasoning

## Current Work

I am developing **ScholarGraph**, a GraphRAG system that gives Claude Code persistent memory of research literature. This enables semantic search over a 51-paper scoping review corpus with automatic gap analysis.
